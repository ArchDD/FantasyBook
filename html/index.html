What is WeStory?

WeStory is an event-driven, multiplayer roleplaying game, where you can join in with other players to play through a story of your choice. Create a character and see it appear on the pages of your books, where its fate is in the hands of you and your fellow players.

Story Progression

Stories progress through taking decisions on events, with a new event on every page. Control of event choices rotates between players on a turn basis. When an event is completed, it is replaced by the resulting story, and a new event becomes available on the next page.

Available Themes

Each book's chosen theme determines the style of story that unfolds. Whether the book is fantasy with mythical creatures and races, or science fiction with spaceships and star systems, is completely up to you.
