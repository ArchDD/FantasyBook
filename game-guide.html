Getting Started

To keep track of the books and characters you create, you should first create an account and login, which can be done by clicking on the 'Login/Register' button in the top-right hand corner.

Characters

As soon as you sign up, a default character is created for you. You must choose how your character should react to different book events, changing the course of the story as you do so.

Clicking on the 'Character' button in the navigation bar above will take you to the Character screen, where you can name and customize your character.

Story Progression

Stories progress through taking decisions on events, with a new event on every page. Control of event choices rotates between players on a turn basis. When an event is completed, it is replaced by the resulting story, and a new event becomes available on the next page.

Book Themes

Each book's chosen theme determines the style of story that unfolds. Whether the book is fantasy with mythical creatures and races, or science fiction with spaceships and star systems, depends entirely on the category chosen when creating the book.
